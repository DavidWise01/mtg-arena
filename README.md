# MTG ARENA · MTG · UD0

**Magic: The Gathering Arena** — the digital client (WotC, 2018) for the first and deepest
trading card game (Richard Garfield, 1993) — catalogued into **UD0** as a game-world.

Standing template: The Arc (1993 → Arena) · **The Color Pie** (the WUBRG philosophy wheel —
five complete worldviews, none good or evil) · **Real or Fluff** (the honest take on the
controversies) · The Message. 15 emergents, full `.dlw` each, tinted to their mana colour:
the five colours + the wheel, the engine (mana, the stack, the battlefield, the planeswalker),
and the Arena layer (the client, the shuffler, the economy, the formats, the metagame).

Honest verdicts: the **shuffler** is *mostly fluff* (Bo3 is fair; Bo1's hand-smoothing is real
& disclosed; the rest is variance memory); **pay-to-win** is really *pay-to-fast* (draft
equalises); and Magic is **provably Turing-complete** — you can build a computer inside a legal
game (Churchill, Biderman & Herrick, 2019).
